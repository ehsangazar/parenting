**[English]**

The last few months have likely felt like a whirlwind of transition as the early foundations of symbolic thought and the first vertical attempts at standing began to take root. There is a specific, bittersweet quality to this threshold, where the infant of the first year is rapidly being replaced by a purposeful walker whose memory and intent are deepening by the day. The physical proximity of early infancy is giving way to a more complex, elastic form of connection that requires a different kind of parental witness.

![Standing alone at the center of the room, looking out — the first sovereign presence in open space](learning/raised-logic/l5-welcome/body-opening-0ba74c79-c057-4423-8dfe-f990e5d8c5ca.png)

This transition marks the moment your child opens their own **Laboratory of the Will**. For the first year, much of their growth was about learning to react to and absorb the environment, but now, a systemic shift toward **Tertiary Circular Reactions** has begun. Think of this as the birth of the active scientist. They are no longer simply repeating an action to see a result; they are now varying that action—dropping a spoon from three different heights, or pushing a ball with different levels of force—to see how the world responds to their specific variables. This is the emergence of **Intentional Choice**, where the brain moves from reactive discovery to purposeful mastery.

As this internal simulator for planning and testing reality matures, it often creates a new kind of developmental friction. The brain is so focused on executing its first high-fidelity plans that transitions can feel like a direct interruption of a vital experiment. A sudden surge in determination or a localized resistance to being helped is usually the symptom of an engine working at max capacity to integrate these new coordinate systems. This intense focus is the energetic cost of building a self-reliant mind that refuses to be a passive passenger in its own life.

You are now stepping into the role of the **Curator of Autonomy**. Your presence remains the essential anchor of security, but the arena is changing from a place of simple care to a landscape of curated options. By tending the environment so that their natural drive for agency can flourish safely, you are providing the high-fidelity data their brain needs to confirm its own rising power. Honoring the effort of their first plans ensures that their movement into the second year is one of confidence and deep internal security.
