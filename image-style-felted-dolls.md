# Raised Image System (Diverse Felted Dolls)

**Current Default:** A tactile, hand-crafted needle-felted wool sculpture style that celebrates human diversity through its materials. This system replaces fixed character descriptions with a modular "Fiber Palette," allowing for a wide representation of skin tones, hair textures, and physical abilities while maintaining a cohesive 3D miniature diorama aesthetic.

---

## Core Principles

- **Medium:** "Needle-felted wool sculpture," "3D fiber art," and "hand-crafted felt miniature."
- **Tactility:** Every figure must showcase a highly tactile "fuzzy wool texture" and a "soft matte finish."
- **Character Style:** "Chibi style," "stylized 3D characters," "kawaii aesthetics," "minimalist facial features."

## Mandatory Tonal Palette — "Waldorf Garden" (Ultra-Pastel)

This is a **tonal consistency rule**, not a color-assignment guide. Every colour that appears anywhere in the image — characters, clothing, environment, props, accents — must belong to the same **ultra-pastel** family. All colours should look as though they have been **mixed with 50% white** — sun-bleached, airy, and washed-out. The goal is that the entire image feels like one harmonious, handmade world bathed in soft morning light.

### The 6 Core Tones + White
| Tone | Ultra-Pastel Description | Approx. Hex |
|---|---|---|
| **Terracotta Clay** | Soft, muted, washed-out earthy clay — not rich or saturated | `#D4A08A` |
| **Sage Leaf** | Very light grey-sage — desaturated and dusty, not vivid green | `#C2CEAF` |
| **Powder Sky** | Ultra-pale, almost-white powder blue | `#D3E4EE` |
| **Warm Cream** | Soft oatmeal, warm linen, natural off-white | `#F5ECD7` |
| **Kraft Paper** | Light sandy tan — like sun-faded cardboard | `#D4C4A8` |
| **Dusty Rose** | Very pale blush pink — like dried petals | `#E0C4C4` |
| **Warm White** | The base light — slightly warm, not stark | `#FDFAF5` |

### Translucency & Lightness Rule
- **Luminous, low-pigment fibers:** The wool should not look like dense, heavy, opaque felt. Instead, it must look almost **translucent**, as if soft light is passing through the fibers themselves.
- **Ethereal, high-key pastel:** Every colour must appear to be dyed with an extremely light, delicate hand — very airy and luminous. 
- No colour should appear "heavy," at full saturation, or mid-tone depth.
- If you squint at the image, it should feel like a soft, glowing, sun-bleached watercolour, not a deeply pigmented photograph.

Any additional colour not listed above is acceptable **only if** it shares the same ultra-light, warm, washed-out pastel quality. No colour should feel like it comes from a different visual world.

### Brightness & Mood Rule
- **Always bright, warm, and cheerful.** The overall image should feel like a sunlit handmade scene.
- **Soft, diffused, natural-looking lighting.** Avoid dramatic shadows or theatrical spotlights.
- ⚠️ **Never use:** Dark/black backgrounds, moody or high-contrast lighting, deep shadow pools, vivid or saturated colours, neon, harsh primaries, or any mid-tone that hasn't been lightened.

## Environment & Cinematography

- **Set Design:** "Hand-crafted diorama," "miniature movie set," "stop-motion background." The specific scene varies per lesson topic.
- **Environmental Materials:** Scenery should be constructed from physical-looking materials like felted wool, cardboard, fabric, stitched thread — but the specific elements depend on the lesson metaphor.

## Modular Prompt Framework

To generate a diverse scene, define characters by their core material traits:

### Template Structure:
```text
[SUBJECT METAPHOR: e.g. A family walking together across a fabric bridge over a river of blue wool.]

Character A: [Role: e.g. Mother] with [Skin Tone token] and [Hair Texture token] wearing [Clothes description].
Character B: [Role: e.g. Child] with [Skin Tone token] and [Hair Texture token] wearing [Clothes description].

CRITICAL COLOR NOTE: The wool fibers must appear luminous, low-pigment, and almost translucent, as if soft light is passing through them. Do not use dense, heavy, or opaque pigments. All colors must be ultra-pastel, airy, and glowing.

Style Anchor: Needle-felted wool sculpture, 3D fiber art, hand-crafted felt miniature. Chibi style, stylized 3D characters, minimalist facial features. Luminous low-pigment wool, translucent tactile fiber surface. Ultra-pastel Waldorf Garden palette: very pale terracotta clay, desaturated grey-sage green, ultra-pale powder-blue sky, warm cream linen, light kraft tan, pale dusty rose accents, warm white. All colours washed-out, high-key, and softly glowing. Hand-crafted diorama set design, stop-motion aesthetic. Macro photography, soft diffused natural-looking studio lighting, shallow depth of field, tilt-shift photography. Ethereally light, airy, cheerful, sunlit mood. No text.
```
